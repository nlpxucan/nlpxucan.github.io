# Bio
* I am a research scientist of Microsoft STCA (Software Technology Center at Asia) NLP Group, working with <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/djiang/">Dr. Daxin Jiang</a> on dialogue systems. Before that, I worked with <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/wei-wu-homepage">Dr. Wei Wu</a> on chatbot systems at Microsoft XiaoIce Research Team from 2018 to 2020 and at Microsoft Research Asia from 2017 to 2018. I am proficient in building open-domain chatbot and contribute core deep models for chat engines of Microsoft XiaoIce (微软小冰) v5,v6,v8 and Microsoft Rinna (りんな). More details, please refer to Publications tab and Projects tab.
* My research interests lie in natural language processing, chatbot systems, and information retrieval.
* I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science from [Sichuan University](http://http://scu.edu.cn/) and Master's Degree in Computer Technology from [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/).
